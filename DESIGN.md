# OncAccess Demo Reel — Design

## Identity
- Background (app): `#f8fafc`  ·  Background (intro/outro): `#0a0a0b`
- Ink: `#0f172a` / soft `#475569` / muted `#94a3b8`
- Teal accent: `#0d9488` (deep `#0f766e`, soft `#ccfbf1`)
- Coral (Patient Pays): `#f87171`
- Green (Govt / Available): `#10b981`
- Insurer blue: `#93c5fd`
- Highlight (focus rings on changing UI): `#facc15`

## Type
- Display: **Manrope** (300 / 400 / 700 / 800)
- Data / labels: **JetBrains Mono** (400 / 700)

## Structure
1. Black opener — three-line indictment
2. Black — "OncAccess brings them together" *(shader: cinematic-zoom into the app)*
3. Landing — typing animation: "Drug A"
4. Indication modal — selection lands on Endometrial Carcinoma
5. Executive Dashboard — Out of Pocket
6. **Three payer realities** — OOP → Government Scheme → Corporate Insurance, with yellow highlight rings on the **Payer pill** and **Cycle Cash Flow chart** as they update
7. War Room · Competitive Thunderdome
8. War Room · Discontinuation Cliff
9. War Room · Deal Architect
10. War Room · Compare My TPP — typing "Drug B" into competitor field
11. War Room · Patient Bridge — predictable EMI vs volatile traditional therapies *(shader: cross-warp-morph to closing)*
12. Black closing — "Your drug. Your deal. Your terms."

## Drug masking
All proprietary drug names are masked. Branded mentions become `Drug A` (the asset) and `Drug B` (the competitor).

## Shader transitions
Two only — both at narrative beats:
- **7.75s**  cinematic-zoom  · s2 → s3 (entering the app)
- **54.75s** cross-warp-morph · s11 → s12 (return to black)

Everything else is hard cut.
